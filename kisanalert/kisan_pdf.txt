The Dual Crisis Facing Marathwada Farmers  
Small and marginal farmers in Maharashtra — who make up over 80% of the state's agricultural population — 
are trapped in a dual crisis of climate volatility and market failure.  
In 2024 alone, 2,706 farmers in Vidarbha and Marathwada died by suicide, with debt from distress selling as 
the primary driver. Unseasonal rains, hailstorms, and recurring droughts destroy crop quality and force early 
harvesting, flooding mandis at the wor st possible time.  
The core gap: Data exists everywhere. Agmarknet has daily prices. IMD has weather. CBOT has global 
commodity prices. The problem is that no system connects these signals into a single actionable warning in 
Marathi — before the crash happens.

Why KisanAlert Cannot Be Easily Copied  
A moat is a competitive advantage difficult for rivals to replicate. Each moat below is backed by concrete 
technical implementation and a specific user benefit no existing agri -tech app provides.  
 
Moat 01 — Lead -Lag Predictive Engine  
Hyper -local price arbitrage — 48-hour head start  
Them  Shows current Latur price: ₹4,200. Shows Nanded price: ₹4,650. No connection 
made. Farmer has zero warning.  
Us "Latur mandi price fell ₹600 today. Based on 5 -year lag pattern, Nanded price will fall 
₹400 -500 in 36 -48 hours. Sell tonight or go to Osmanabad tomorrow morning."  
Tech  LSTM trained on inter -district price correlations from 5 -year Agmarknet history. 
Learns exact lag time per crop per district pair. Feature: price_wave_lag_score.  
 
Moat 02 — Climate -to-Cash Risk Logic  
Weather + price merged into ONE rupee -impact action  
Them  Tab 1: "Rain tomorrow 38mm." Tab 2: "Turmeric price ₹12,400." No connection. 
Farmer must guess combined impact himself.  
Us "पाऊस  24 तासाांत  येणार  आणण  भाि आत्ता  जास्त  आहेत  — आत्ताच  काढा  आणण  विका . 
थाांबल्यास  ₹4,400 प्रतत ज्िांटल  नुकसान होईल ." Farmer gets exact rupee impact.  
Tech  Fusion rule: rain_forecast_mm × harvest_window_flag × (current_price/30day_avg) = 
combined_urgency_score. Gemini API generates Marathi action with rupee estimate.  
 
Moat 03 — Offline -First Edge Computing  
Works in Gadchiroli with zero 4G/5G signal  
Them  BharatAgri Play Store review: "Shows Internet not working even though connection is 
fine." MahaVISTAAR requires active internet for every function.  
Us TFLite pest model (8MB) runs on device. Last 48hr price prediction cached locally. 
Map tiles pre -cached 50km radius. Farmer gets instant AI in middle of a field at 6 AM 
with zero signal.

3 Models Working Together — Complete Workflow  
Each model reads different signals. Their scores are combined into one crash probability using a weighted 
ensemble. A hard rule engine can override all ML scores instantly for sudden policy shocks.  
 
Model 1 — LSTM (Long Short -Term Memory)  
Best for sequential patterns over time. Reads the last 30 days of price + arrival + weather as a sequence and 
learns "after 3 consecutive days of rising arrivals + rain forecast, price drops on day 4."  
Parameter  Value  Reason  
Architecture  2-layer LSTM → Dense(64) 
→ Dense(1, sigmoid)  Deep enough to learn 30 -day patterns 
without overfitting small Nanded dataset  
Sequence length  30 days sliding window  Captures full seasonal micro -cycle + 
weather build -up period  
Input features  18 features per timestep  All 7 factors converted to numeric signals 
(see feature list below)  
Output heads  3 outputs: 1 -day, 3 -day, 7 -
day crash probability  Separate predictions for each time 
horizon  
Loss function  Binary cross -entropy + class 
weight 3:1  Crashes are rare events — upweight 
them during training  
Training data  Nanded soybean 2019 -2022 
(~1,095 rows)  District -specific — not generic India 
model. Local patterns = higher accuracy  
Validation  2023 (365 rows)  Time -series split — no data leakage from 
future into training  
Test (backtest)  2024 (365 rows)  Real -world proof — did it predict Sep 
2024 crash?  
Target accuracy  78-85% crash detection  Consistent with MDPI 2025 research 
benchmarks for LSTM on Agmarknet data
